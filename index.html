HOW TO PLAY

  • The aim of this little dice game is to reach a score of 50
  • Player 1 begins by rolling the dice. The value of the roll is added to their holding score. Player 1 continues to roll until they are happy with their holding score. Press the hold button to add this score to their bank.
  • But BEWARE Rolling a ONE takes your holding score back down to zero. And Player 2 begins their turn.
  • The first player to reach a score of 50 wins the game.
